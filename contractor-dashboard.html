4. Compensation & Payment Terms

• Contractor is compensated on a flat-rate, per-job basis, agreed upon prior to job acceptance

• Approved payment methods include Zelle, Venmo, or CashApp, as designated by the Company

• Contractor must submit all required documentation prior to payment, including:

• Before and after photos (if required)

• Completed checklists (if applicable)

• Incident or damage reports (if applicable)

• A valid W-9 on file

Payment Timing

Payment timelines vary by job type:

Residential Jobs

• Payment is typically issued within 24–48 hours following:

• Verified job completion

• Approval of required documentation

• Client payment confirmation

Commercial, Multi-Unit, Make-Ready, or Property Management Jobs

• Payment may be issued within 15–30 days following:

• Verified completion of services

• Client or property management approval

• Company receipt of payment

Contractor acknowledges that commercial and property management clients operate on net billing cycles, and payment is contingent upon client remittance.

Withheld or Delayed Payments

The Company reserves the right to delay or withhold payment if:

• Rework or corrections are required

• Required documentation is missing or incomplete

• Property damage, loss, or theft is under investigation

• Client payment is delayed, disputed, or subject to chargeback

Payment will be released once all issues are resolved in accordance with this Agreement.

• Jobs are claimed on a first-come basis

Reliability and consistency are essential to maintaining active contractor status.
