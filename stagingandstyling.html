Staging & styling FAQ
Do you bring your own furniture?
For most occupied homes, we primarily use your existing furniture and layer decor, textiles, and styling pieces. For vacant or partially furnished homes, we can discuss options and collaborate with trusted partners if full furniture staging is needed.
Can you stage my home if I’m still living in it?
Absolutely. Occupied staging is one of our most requested services. We’ll create a plan that works with your day-to-day life while still presenting the home beautifully for buyers.
What does pricing look like?
Every home and timeline is different, so we start with a walkthrough to see the space in person. From there, we provide a detailed quote based on square footage, number of rooms, and the level of styling needed.
Can you coordinate with my photographer or realtor?
Yes. We’re happy to communicate directly with your agent or photographer about timing, lighting, and any priority shots so staging and photos work seamlessly together.
