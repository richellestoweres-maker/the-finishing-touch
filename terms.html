Client Service Terms

Updated: Sep 10, 2025

Scheduling & Access

  • We’ll confirm your appointment window by email/SMS. Please ensure building/door codes and pets are noted in your intake.
  • If we cannot access your home within 15 minutes of arrival, the visit may be cancelled and a fee may apply.

Cancellation & Rescheduling

  • Please give at least 24 hours’ notice to cancel or reschedule. Same-day cancellations or lockouts may incur a fee up to the minimum service charge.

Safety & Working Conditions

  • For everyone’s safety, we decline hazardous conditions (active infestations, biohazards, aggressive animals, etc.).
  • We cannot move heavy or fragile items without your direction; we are not responsible for pre-existing damage.

Photos (Quality Control & Marketing)

  • We may capture before/after photos strictly for job verification and quality control.
  • Marketing use is optional and only with your separate consent in the intake form.

Privacy & Communications

  • We use your contact information solely to provide services and updates about scheduling and your project.
  • By opting in, you agree we may contact you via email/SMS with updates and reminders.
  • Staffing transparency: Our services are provided by carefully selected independent contractors who have completed background checks and meet our professional standards. They represent The Finishing Touch while on the job.

Supplies & Scope

  • We bring professional products and tools. Let us know about any product sensitivities or surfaces requiring special care.
  • Scope is guided by your intake and the service type booked (Initial, Standard, Deep, Move-In/Out, etc.).

Organizing Addendum (if applicable)

  • We sort, categorize, and set up systems in collaboration with you.
  • Donations & discards: Clients are responsible for removing donations and discards. We’re happy to consolidate into bags/boxes and stage for your preferred pickup or drop-off.

Payment

  • Payment is due at completion unless otherwise agreed. We accept Zelle, Venmo, Cash App, and Cash.
  • If you elect to put a card on file (optional), it is stored securely by our payment provider and may be charged after service per your authorization.

Satisfaction

  • If anything was missed, please notify us within 24 hours so we can make it right.
