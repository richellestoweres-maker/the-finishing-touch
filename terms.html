Client Terms

  • Scheduling. Appointments are booked via our online calendar. Start times are windows; we’ll text/email the day before with any refinements.
  • Access. Please ensure entry (door code, lockbox, or someone present). If we cannot gain access within 15 minutes of arrival, the visit may be canceled as a no-show.
  • Preparation. A quick tidy helps us focus on the service tasks. Please secure pets and disclose any hazards (loose steps, allergies, etc.).
  • Scope. Work performed follows your intake selection and our checklists. Add-ons (e.g., oven, inside fridge, bins/labels, garland) must be listed in the intake or approved onsite.
  • Time blocks. We book by time. If conditions differ from intake (extra buildup, clutter, access constraints), we may recommend a larger block or split service across visits.
  • Payment. Payment is due at service completion unless otherwise arranged. We accept Zelle, Venmo, Cash App, or cash. Card on file (optional) will be authorized but not charged until service is completed.
  • Cancellation & Rescheduling.
  • Client cancels/reschedules ≥ 24 hours: No fee.
  • Client cancels/reschedules < 24 hours: $50 late-change fee.
  • No-show or no access: $75 fee.
  • Contractor reschedule: We notify ASAP and rebook the earliest comparable window.
  • Breakage & Satisfaction. Please report concerns within 24 hours with photos. We’ll return to address missed items or discuss a fair remedy. Normal wear, pre-existing damage, or unstable fixtures aren’t covered.
  • Safety. We reserve the right to stop or refuse service if conditions are unsafe (biohazards, aggressive animals, harassment). A fee may apply for lost time.
  • Allergies & Products. Tell us about sensitivities in your intake. We use professional-grade products; client-supplied products are at client’s risk.

Organizing Addendum

  • Client participation. Decision-making speed affects scope. If decisions are deferred, we’ll focus on sorting/zone setup and may recommend a follow-up.
  • Containers & supplies. We can recommend or shop for bins/labels per your budget. Purchases are billed at cost; time spent sourcing is part of your block.
  • Haul-away. We can consolidate donations; limited haul-away is available by request (volume limits/fees may apply) or we’ll coordinate a pickup partner.

Holiday Decorating Addendum (Indoor Only)

  • Scope. Indoor trees, mantles, stair garlands, wreaths, tablescapes, and vignettes. Outdoor installs are not offered at this time.
  • Materials. Client-provided décor is preferred. We can source items to your approved budget. Materials remain the client’s property.
  • Trees & ladders. For trees ≥ 9ft, secure bases are required. We may use step-ladders; unsafe scenarios will be adjusted or declined.
  • Teardown. Optional teardown is quoted at ~60% of install hours; labeled storage is available on request.

Photo Consent

  • Documentation photos (internal). Before/after photos are used privately for quality control, estimating, and job records.
  • Marketing photos (optional). With explicit consent, select images (no personal info shown) may be shared on our site or social media. You can withdraw marketing consent at any time.

Privacy & Communications

  • Contact methods. By providing your email/phone, you agree to receive scheduling updates and service messages. Standard carrier rates apply; opt out any time.
  • Data. We store intake details and service history to serve you better. We never sell your data.
  • Payments. If you elect to save a card on file, processing is handled securely by our PCI-compliant provider. We do not store card numbers on our site.

Contractor Terms (For Subcontractors)

Applies to independent contractors who accept jobs from The Finishing Touch.

  • Independent status. Contractors operate as 1099 independent businesses and are responsible for their own taxes, tools, and transportation.
  • Background & eligibility. Consent to a background check and provide proof of general liability insurance prior to accepting work.
  • Compensation. Flat-rate or hourly equivalent disclosed per job. Standard split: 70% contractor / 30% company, unless otherwise agreed in writing.
  • Acceptance. You may accept/decline jobs. Once accepted, on-time arrival and professional conduct are required. Communicate issues immediately.
  • Quality & photos. Follow the provided checklist and submit clear before/after photos for verification and payment processing.
  • Non-solicit. For 12 months after your last job, do not directly solicit or accept work from clients you were introduced to through us, outside of our platform.
